This is your Time
"AVENIR" is a gadget which grants user the power to travel across different timelines. This is the power to travel into the future or into the past.
In the year 2027, a person named "Hector" claimed to be one of the time travelers. But none of the people believed him and thus considered him to be mentally affected. But one day he suddenly disappeared. He was nowhere to be found. After some intense searching in his house a box was found. This box contained a wrist watch type gadget with a certain note. The note read "To everyone who thought me to be a liar, this is a solid proof of me being a time traveler. 'AVENIR' is a gadget which allows one to defy the natural laws and makes it possible to time-travel. Consider this as a parting gift and handle it with care."
After some intense research humanity has finally been able to replicate these gadgets and the use of gadgets is now done using a Website called "AVENIR-WEB"
